Columbo Cigar
Branding

This brand was created to support the idea of the cigar. I desided to use the character of Peter Falk as the detective Columbo to sell the idea of the classic everyday cigar. Using this character also appeals to the target audience more. The insparation for the style and design is Pablo Lobato and his abstract celebrity portraits.

WWF Info Booklet

This booklet is an informational piece that would be handed out to business partners or donars. Since people working with this company are there because they care about our earth and its creatures, the main focus was on full page photography of the animal. To make this piece stand out from other information booklets a partner may have is the slip cover. Taking the dynamic photo of a cat but showing just the eye, leaves a scense of mystery that make one want to dig deeper.
